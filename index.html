A Letter to My Love

My Dearest Mark,

Where do I even begin? Words feel too small for what my heart holds for you. But I'll try, because you deserve to know exactly what you mean to me.

You walked into my life quietly, and somehow, you became the loudest, most beautiful thing I've ever felt. In a world full of noise, you are my silence. In the chaos, you are my calm. You didn't just become part of my life—you became the reason I want to live it fully.

I want to thank you. Thank you for choosing me, even when it wasn't easy. Thank you for staying when you could have left. Thank you for seeing me—really seeing me—and loving me anyway. Thank you for every laugh, every conversation that lasted until dawn, every moment where the world felt like it was just us.

I promise you this: My commitment to you isn't just a choice I made once. It's a choice I make every single day. It's in the small things—the way I think of you first, the way your happiness matters more than anything else. It's in the big things—standing beside you through every storm, believing in your dreams as if they were my own.

You are my peace. Not because everything is easy, but because with you, I'm not afraid of hard things. You've shown me what it means to be truly loyal, and I promise to give you the same in return. Forever isn't just a word between us—it's a vow.

I'm so grateful for you. For your heart, your strength, your kindness, your humor, your presence. You make me want to be better. You inspire me. You complete me in ways I didn't know I needed.

As I look toward our future, I see endless possibilities. I see us growing old together, building memories that will fill a lifetime. I see love that deepens with each passing year. I see a forever that's not just real—it's extraordinary.

Mark, you are my greatest blessing. You are my hope. You are my heart.

I love you more than words could ever express. Not just today, not just tomorrow—but always. Through every season, every challenge, every beautiful ordinary moment.

Yours completely,

Godfrey ❤️
